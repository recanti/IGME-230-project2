Conway's Game of Life

This is a side-project made using the HTML5 Canvas. Having never used the canvas before, I made this in order to get familiar with it. It was also one of my first projects to use a significant amount of Javascript.

In terms of style, I wanted it to have an old-school video game feel. I used BLUEamnesiac's Game Boy Pocket color palettes.
