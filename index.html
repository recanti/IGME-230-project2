About Me

I have always been passionate about art and technology. For as long as I can remember, I have been drawing and playing with cool gadgets. For me, working on projects that combine these two interests is the most rewarding experience

I think the web is one of the best places to explore these possibilities. Different design trends are constantly emerging, and there's always a different piece of technology to learn about. Plus, it's fun and easy to create new things and share them with the world

In between working on projects, I am also an avid drinker of coffee. When I am not working, I enjoy running, cooking, and drawing.
